About the Conference

The Peace Education Conference on Drug Abuse gathers students and the general public to share knowledge about substance misuse prevention, community support, and recovery pathways. Our goals:

  • Raise awareness about the health and social effects of drug abuse.
  • Provide practical prevention strategies for schools and communities.
  • Reduce stigma and connect people to support and resources.

Organizers welcome NGOs, community leaders, teachers, health workers, and youth groups.
